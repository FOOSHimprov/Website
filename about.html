About Us
Who are we?
We are UCSD's first ever (and only) short-form improvisational comedy troupe. We were founded in 2003 to build a community of people who love comedy and performing. Although we are based out of Muir College, we are open to any current student at UCSD, whether they have any experience in improv or not! We come together at least twice a week throughout the academic year to better ourselves and improve our improv, while also creating life-long friendships and a mutual trust between improvisers.
What do we do?
Our primary goal is learning, teaching, and practicing improv comedy skills. We hold practices twice a week led by a coach who is responsible for helping members learn what they need to do in order to be successful at improv comedy. Throughout the academic year, we hold approximately fifteen performances, during which select members of the club, chosen by the executive board, will perform in front of a live audience and test out the skills they've been practicing. Those shows are all held on-campus and are completely free to attend!
