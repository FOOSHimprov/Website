FAQ
How often are shows?
Shows are typically every other week. For specific information regarding our currently planned show schedule, check out our shows page! If you want to be kept completely up to date on upcoming shows, you can subcribe to our email list!
Who can join?
The club is open to any current UCSD student!
How can I join?
To join, all you have to do is attend one of our workshops at the beginning of either fall or winter quarter. Details on currently planned workshops can be found on the homepage. If you want to be kept completely up to date on workshop schedules and information, you can also subscribe to our email list!
When and where are practices?
Practice is held twice a week, every Monday and Wednesday night from 8-10pm, in the Mariposa Room in Muir.
I'm having an event and I want FOOSH to perform. What do I do?
FOOSH usually prefers to perform only at FOOSH-hosted events, but we do sometimes make exceptions. If you'd like us to perform at your event, send us an email using the form on our contact page and we will get back to you as soon as we can!
